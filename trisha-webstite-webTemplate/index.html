The Adventures of Tim Bur

By Trisha Islam

Meet Tim, he had planned the perfect summer getaway to get out of the city and have a chance to reconnect with himself and nature. Unfortunately Tim's plans got ruined when Covid-19 forced every nation to go under lockdown. With nowhere to go, reluctant Tim is forced to make the most of his time at home.
This video was made to convey the fact that while things may not always go as planned,
sometimes the divertion may also be good for us
